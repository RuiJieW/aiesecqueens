OUR MISSION

Leadership & Professional Development

AIESEC’s Global Leader program is an experiential learning and development platform created for youth, by youth. We are a global movement of “do-ers”, giving those who participate in our programs – Global Citizen, Global Talent, and Global Internship Program – the unique advantage of being a part of something bigger than themselves.

Our generation has the opportunity to build a world filled with opportunities for all that live in it. The youth of today needs practical experience, an international mindset, and leadership skills.
